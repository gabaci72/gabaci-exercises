Green Thumb Tracking Club

Let's meet up. Let's exercise. But only celebrate those who put in the effort.

Let's stop at famous places, have a snack while we rest, and share the food we have.

Green Thumb Tracking Club is a free MeetUp group sponsored by City Shoes Center.

"I make my plans around their activities. I can combine nature exploration and exercise every week and meet new people!"

-Mike M. - Realtor/newbie tracker since 2010

Come join the fun!

We meet every Sunday morning in the city square at 7AM!
